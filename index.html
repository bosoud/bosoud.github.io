A sleek, modern personal website theme

Personal has been designed to put your content first, and is the perfect Jekyll theme to use for your personal website. We’ve included everything you need to create engaging posts about your work, and show off your case studies in a beautiful way.

Features

Personal is full of powerful features to help showcase your work:

Beautiful, minimal design

Personal has been designed to put your content first, with a stylish and minimal interface to make navigation easy.

Single column layout

A wide, single column layout gives you plenty of space to show off your images and make the most of the screen.

Responsive design

Personal works perfectly on all devices, adapting the layout to fit best with whatever screen size it's viewed on.

Portfolio post type

A dedicated post type for your portfolio items, helping you to keep things organized.

Blog section

Personal includes a section for your blog, with simple pagination features to reduce load times and let your visitors explore your older posts.

Ajax navigation

The sleek Ajax navigation and page transitions help give that 'premium' feel that your site deserves.

Image galleries

We've created a unique ‘gallery’ feature for image grids and carousels, making it easy to add these to your posts with some simple HTML.

Contact form

Built-in contact form with Formspree integration for free form submissions.

Markdown + code formatting

Personal has full markdown support, and built-in code highlighting to make sure your code samples look as great as the rest of your content.

Social media icons

We've built in social media icons for your favorite networks, making it easy to link to your external profiles.

SEO friendly

We built Personal with SEO best practices in mind, and a focus on fast page rendering.

Customize everything

It's easy to change colors, spacing, fonts, sizes etc. via a simple settings file – you don't even need to touch the code.

Works with GitHub Pages

Host your site for free on GitHub Pages, no non-native plugins needed.
