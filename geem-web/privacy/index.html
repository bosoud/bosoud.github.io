Privacy Policy — Geem

Last updated: 2026-05-07 · bosoud.com
Englishالعربية

Overview

Geem (the “app”) is an Arabic trivia and party game published by Abdulwahab Alkharaz (“we,” “us,” “Bosoud”). This page explains what data the app collects, why, and what you can do about it.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, address, or any free-text you type into the app (game names, custom category content, team names, player names, custom questions).
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites. We do not use Apple’s Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA).
  • Game audio, video, photos imported from your device, and your custom content stay on your device.

Data we do collect

To improve Geem’s reliability and understand which features matter, the app uses Google Firebase Analytics and Google Firebase Crashlytics. These services collect:

  • A pseudonymous device identifier (Firebase Installation ID — distinct from Apple’s IDFA) so we can group events from the same install. Never linked to your name, email, or App Store account.
  • In-app interactions — eight aggregate event types: app install, first game completion, paywall view, paywall dismiss, subscription purchase, pack purchase, share invocation, and a daily heartbeat. We do not collect game answers, custom content, or any free text.
  • Crash and performance data — stack traces, device model, OS version, and app version when the app crashes, plus launch and foreground timing.

Data is processed by Google on our behalf in their global infrastructure under Google’s privacy commitments to Firebase developers. See Google Firebase data processing terms and Google Privacy Policy.

Purchases

In-app purchases (subscription, game packs, single-game pass) are processed by Apple’s App Store. We never see your payment details. Apple sends us anonymized purchase confirmations so we can unlock your purchases on your device. See Apple’s Privacy Policy.

iCloud

Your game credit balance is backed up to your private iCloud Key-Value Store so it survives reinstalls on devices signed into your Apple ID. The data stays in your iCloud account; we do not have access to it.

Buzzer mode

Buzzer mode uses Apple’s MultipeerConnectivity over your local Wi‑Fi to connect phones in the same room. Only the host phone runs the game; player phones send button taps. No data leaves your local network during a buzzer game.

Children

The app is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from children. The data Firebase collects is technical (device ID, crash logs) and contains no identifying information about who is using the device.

Your rights

You can request deletion of all Firebase telemetry associated with your device by emailing doooshxp@gmail.com with the subject “Delete my Geem data” and a description of the device involved. Firebase data is also automatically deleted by Google after 14 months of inactivity.

Changes

If we change this policy materially we will update the date at the top and, where reasonable, post a notice in the app. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

Contact

Questions, concerns, or data deletion requests: doooshxp@gmail.com.
